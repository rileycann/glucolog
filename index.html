GlucoLog
T1D management, your way

A personal log that learns your patterns — less data entry, smarter dose guidance, and no more mental load from repetitive tracking.

Talk to it like a person

Type anything: "had oatmeal 80g, banana, coffee at 8am" or "6u NovoRapid" or "going for a 45 min bike ride." It handles the structure.

Learns your patterns

After a few weeks of data, GlucoLog computes your insulin-to-carb ratios by time of day and flags how fat, exercise, and substances affect your response.

Your data, your device

Everything lives locally in your browser. Export to JSON any time. No accounts, no cloud sync required.
